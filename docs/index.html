StudentBioMetrics

A Health Informatics CLI tool for international students balancing study, sleep, stress, and shift work.

View on GitHub

The Problem

You are an international student. You study all day, work night shifts, sleep too little, and feel stressed. But how bad is it really? StudentBioMetrics gives you a data-driven answer — right from your terminal.

Features

Daily Logging

Log sleep, study, breaks, stress, shift hours and productivity in one command.

Health Scoring

Auto-calculates your daily health score based on behavioral metrics.

Weekly Reports

Get a weekly summary of your average sleep, stress, study and health trend.

Burnout Detection

Detects if you are stable, at risk, or heading toward burnout.

Data Export

Export your data as JSON or CSV for further analysis.

Fast and Offline

No internet needed. Runs locally. Data stays on your machine.

How It Works

# Log your day
studentbiometrics log --sleep 6 --study 4 --breaks 1 --stress 3 --shift 5

# Get your weekly report
studentbiometrics weekly-report

# See daily trends
studentbiometrics trends

# Export your data
studentbiometrics export --format json
